# TripConnect

TripConnect is a travel-focused social platform that combines the community aspects of social media with practical travel planning tools. Users can share their travel experiences, discover destinations through authentic traveler insights, and access detailed trip information to help plan their own journeys.

## Overview

Inspired by modern social networking platforms, TripConnect enables travelers to create rich travel posts that go beyond photos and videos. Each post can include detailed travel information such as itineraries, budgets, accommodation details, transportation options, attractions visited, and travel tips.

The platform aims to become a trusted source of real-world travel experiences, helping users make informed decisions based on community-generated content and transparent ratings.

## Key Features

### Travel Experience Sharing

* Create and share travel posts with photos, videos, and descriptions.
* Add complete itineraries for trips and destinations.
* Share detailed budget breakdowns and expense information.
* Include hotel, restaurant, and transportation recommendations.
* Document attractions, activities, and local experiences.

### Privacy Controls

* Public posts visible to the entire community.
* Private posts accessible only to approved followers or selected users.
* Flexible privacy settings for individual trips and content.

### Social Networking

* Follow other travelers and creators.
* Discover new destinations through community content.
* Engage with posts through likes, comments, and interactions.
* Build a personalized travel network.

### Destination Ratings & Reviews

* Rate destinations, hotels, attractions, and experiences.
* Submit detailed reviews based on multiple evaluation criteria.
* Explore community-driven rankings and recommendations.

### Intelligent Ranking System

TripConnect uses a parameter-based rating system combined with Bayesian ranking methods to provide fair and reliable destination rankings.

Ratings can consider multiple factors, including:

* Overall experience
* Value for money
* Safety
* Accessibility
* Accommodation quality
* Food and dining
* Transportation
* Cleanliness
* Family friendliness
* Solo traveler suitability

To prevent manipulation and ensure fairness, rankings are calculated using Bayesian averaging. This approach balances rating quality with rating quantity, ensuring destinations with only a few high ratings do not unfairly outrank destinations with consistently strong reviews from a larger number of travelers.

### Top 100 Destinations

* Dynamic Top 100 destination rankings.
* Category-specific leaderboards.
* Regional and global rankings.
* Rankings updated continuously based on community activity and Bayesian scoring.

## Vision

TripConnect aims to become the world's most trusted community-driven travel platform, where authentic experiences, transparent reviews, and data-driven rankings help travelers discover, plan, and share unforgettable journeys.
